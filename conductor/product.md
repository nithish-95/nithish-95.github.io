# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A personal portfolio website built with Next.js.

## Purpose
The primary purpose of this portfolio website is multifaceted:
- To showcase professional projects and experience to potential employers.
- To serve as a personal blog to share insights and articles.
- To provide a contact point for networking and collaborations.

## Target Audience
The primary audience for this portfolio includes:
- Recruiters and hiring managers in the tech industry.
- Fellow developers and peers in the open-source community.
- Potential clients and freelance opportunities.

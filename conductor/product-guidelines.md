# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The writing style should be a blend of **conversational and professional**. Content should be accessible and engaging for a broad audience, including recruiters, developers, and potential clients, while maintaining a high standard of professionalism and technical accuracy.

## Brand Messaging
The core message should convey expertise, a passion for technology, and a collaborative spirit. The portfolio should position the author as a knowledgeable and approachable professional who is open to new opportunities and discussions.

## Visual Identity
The visual design should be **modern, clean, and user-friendly**. It should reflect the author's personal brand and taste. The UI should be intuitive and easy to navigate, ensuring a pleasant user experience. The use of Shadcn UI components will contribute to a polished and consistent look and feel.
